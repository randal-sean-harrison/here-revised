Wear a Mask

  • Cloth masks are an effective tool to protect against the spread of the virus, especially by asymptomatic individuals. Every faculty, staff, and student who will be on campus will receive 3 cloth masks.
  • All students, faculty, and staff in a University campus space will be required to wear face coverings at all times when they are, or may be, in the presence of other individuals, except when alone in a private room (office, residence hall room) or in a private vehicle. Masks should be worn outdoors when social distance can not be maintained.
  • Face coverings should cover the nose and mouth and fit snugly but comfortably against the face. Wash your hands or use sanitizer prior to handling their mask. Cloth masks should be laundered and machine dried frequently.
  • We encourage all faculty, staff, and students to follow face mask protocols when off-campus and in the local community to fulfill our shared responsibility.
  • Visitors to campus are expected to wear face covering at all times.
  • Those attending public Masses in the Basilica must wear a mask and practice physical distancing before, during, and after Mass, in accordance with the requirements of the Diocese of Fort Wayne-South Bend.
