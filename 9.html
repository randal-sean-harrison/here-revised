Student Life This Fall

Living in a Residence Hall

Residence Halls

  • Undergraduate residence halls will operate at standard occupancy, though significantly enhanced cleaning and other adjustments in common life will be necessary to keep one another safe.
  • Students will not need to wear masks when they are in their assigned rooms, but masks must be worn in all other rooms and common spaces, including hallways and lounges. Hall lounges will be adjusted for physical distancing.
  • Guests will be permitted in residence halls, though they may not stay overnight. Guests must wear masks and observe physical distancing at all times and in all spaces.

Kitchen and Gym

  • Shared kitchens and hall fitness rooms may be available through appointments or limited hours, dependent on sufficient cleaning and disinfecting.
