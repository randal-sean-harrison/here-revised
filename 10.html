Being a Good Neighbor

Protect and Respect Our Community

  • Serve as a role model and leader in protecting the vulnerable in our community, both on and off campus.
  • Offer respect and kindness to each other as we all work to navigate uncertainty and change.
  • Keep in mind that individuals (both on and off campus) have different levels of comfort with exposure risk, and that it’s impossible to tell just by looking at individuals who may or may not be in a “high risk” category.
  • Practice the highest level of safety precautions in all interactions.
  • When possible, and where health and safety guidelines allow, support local community businesses. Be aware of and follow posted guidelines and directions of the local government and individual businesses when in the community.
