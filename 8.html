Living in Community

Lead with kindness—Remember that we’re accountable to ourselves and each other.

Demonstrate respect and kindness for each other as we all work to navigate uncertainty and change. Serve as a role model and leader in protecting the vulnerable in our greater community.

Maintain Cleanliness

  • Maintain a supply of cleaning materials for your living space.
  • Make it a practice to regularly sanitize high-touch areas such as door knobs.

Maintain Cleanliness

  • Communicate clearly with colleagues, roommates and friends about each other’s level of comfort with different kinds of exposure and risk.
  • Discuss expectations about cleaning and maintaining a healthy environment.
  • Discuss expectations for and comfort with guests or social gatherings.

Maintain Cleanliness

  • Follow health and safety guidelines if you’re thinking about hosting a social gathering of any kind
  • For contact tracing purposes, the host of any gathering should track attendance.
