Our New Normal On Campus

We will provide resources and information across campus to help remind us of the new ways of going about our day.

There is a lot to remember. We’re here to help and remind one another of the new ways we’ll be together on campus. Look for signage with the HERE logo for clear directions on how to navigate spaces and resources. Need assistance? Look for our HERE Ambassadors for one-on-one assistance or contact the Covid-19 Response Unit (CRU) at 631-XXXX.

HERE Signage

The University has developed a portfolio of signage and decals to guide building occupants and visitors as they enter, travel through, occupy, and exit buildings. Classrooms, labs, workspaces, and common areas in buildings such as main entry doors, corridors, lobbies, waiting areas, will be populated with signage with reminders of safe hygiene practices and other important information.

HERE Ambassadors

Located at key locations around campus, ambassadors will inform and assist with new campus practices, recognizing and rewarding students, faculty and staff behaviors.

Re-configured Campus Spaces

Campus spaces have been rearranged and marked to ensure physical distancing. All high-touch surfaces will be cleaned on a daily basis.

Limited Travel, Visitors and Gatherings

Throughout the fall 2020 semester, University-related travel, large gatherings, and campus visitors will be limited and in accordance to public health protocols and guidance.
