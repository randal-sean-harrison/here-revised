Return to Campus Orientation

Creating a safe and healthy environment for the members of the Notre Dame community is a shared responsibility—one that will require each of us to do our part and to be accountable to one another for our behavior, both on and off campus.

As a condition of returning to campus, all Notre Dame faculty, staff, and students are required to complete this brief orientation program (5 sections; <15 minutes) outlining the university's COVID-19 related policies and requirements ensuring a safe return to campus. After reviewing each section and signing the commitment on the last page, you will receive an email acknowledging your completion of the Return to Campus orientation.

Note: This orientation is for all faculty, staff, and students, including those working remotely. Program level training for specialized groups and units (e.g. Chicago office staff) may be provided in addition to this general orientation for the South Bend campus. For additional details and guidelines on the information summarized in this orientation, please visit Here.ND.Edu.
