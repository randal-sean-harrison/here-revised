Before You Begin Your Day

  • All Notre Dame students, faculty and staff) should conduct a daily health check by taking their temperature and assessing symptoms.  Download the Daily Health Screening App (link will be emailed to you upon completing this orientation) or consult with your supervisor about alternative methods for reporting daily health checks.
  • If you have a temperature (above 100.4), any of the symptoms listed below, or if you believe you have been exposed to someone with Covid-19, you should stay home or in your dorm and contact your healthcare provider, or: Students - St. Liam Hall, 574-631-7497; Faculty and Staff - Notre Dame Wellness Center, 574-634-9355. Your healthcare provider will advise you on the appropriate next steps.
  • Please let your supervisor, department chair, or faculty member know that you are unable to report to work or class as expected.
  • A new COVID-19 Response Unit (CRU) has been established on campus, comprised of medically-trained and administrative personnel. The CRU will monitor daily health checks, oversee our onsite testing center (Gate D of Notre Dame Stadium), perform contact tracing for positive COVID-19 cases, manage quarantine and isolation spaces for students in partnership with student affairs, and support a hotline phone number to respond to COVID-19 inquiries.
Symptoms of COVID-19
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Repeated shaking with chills
  • Runny nose or new nasal congestion
  • Muscle pain
  • Headache
  • Sore throat
  • Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea
  • Loss of taste or smell
